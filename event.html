Empowering the African Diaspora Nursing Community

The African-Diaspora Nursing Alliance (A-DNA) is a non-profit organization dedicated to linking and advancing African nurses globally — driving meaningful progress in health outcomes worldwide.

Global Connectivity

We connect African nurses across the diaspora, building a professional network that transcends borders. Through knowledge sharing, mentorship, and collaborative advocacy, we amplify African nursing voices on a truly international scale.

Explore Hub →

Advancement of Nurses

We champion the professional growth of African nurses — addressing a critical gap in the face of a global nursing shortage and the longstanding underrepresentation of African perspectives in healthcare leadership.

Learn More →

Global Health Impact

By elevating African nurses, A-DNA strengthens the communities that need it most. We believe that when African nurses thrive, the world's most underserved populations receive the quality care they deserve.

Mission

We exist to bridge the gap between African nurses at home and abroad — building lasting partnerships that strengthen nursing education across Africa and deliver better health outcomes for African people everywhere.

Vision

We envision an Africa that shapes the future of global nursing — cultivating a world-class workforce of nurses whose expertise transforms health outcomes for communities everywhere.
